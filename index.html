About

A builder, not just a learner.

I'm a young tech enthusiast based in Bangladesh, obsessed with robotics, AI and software that does something real in the physical world. From a line-following robot in 2019 to representing my country at the FIRST Global Challenge in Singapore and Panama, I've spent every year since shipping things — competitions, prototypes, products and websites.

Today I run Miracle Robotics, a venture I founded in 2026 to turn tech into community-scale solutions: factory automation, drone logistics, AI-powered smart homes, flood-resilient infrastructure and smart agriculture. Every prototype on this site exists. The science is sound. What I'm looking for now are partners and investors to help take them to industrial scale.
